History

Experience and history are only developed over time, they cannot be taught. Since 1975 Richard Stone has represented a countless number of clients with a variety of legal issues in Ontario. The firm has established itself as a model for legal performance in the Greater Toronto Area.

One of the core values of our firm is respect for the client. We understand that you as the client are entering an extremely difficult period in life. It is our goal to be empathetic towards that and to demonstrate an understanding from an emotional, as well as a legal, standpoint.

Our firm prides itself on the results it has brought to clients since establishment. Mr. Stone is able to continually bring these results to clients because he has an in depth understanding and appreciation for the Canadian legal system. Fair and just representation are foundational values of our firm.

A Message From Richard

Hello,

As a lawyer who has been practicing law in Ontario for almost 40 years I know that seeking legal counsel and experiencing legal proceedings can be a trying time for all parties involved. During my career I have discovered that the most important goal I can achieve is putting my client's mind at ease.
